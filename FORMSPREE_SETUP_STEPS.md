## 📋 STEP 1: Create Your Formspree Account & Form

### 1.1 Go to Formspree
Open: **https://formspree.io**

### 1.2 Sign Up
- Click **"Sign Up"** (top right)
- Enter your email (your clinic email)
- Create a strong password
- Click **"Create Account"**
- **Verify your email** (check inbox)

### 1.3 Create a Form
1. After login, click **"Create"** (top left)
2. Select **"Form"**
3. **Name:** `Appointment`
4. **Receiving Email:** Enter your clinic's receptionist email (e.g., `receptionist@dentalclinic.com`)
5. Click **"Create Form"**

### 1.4 Get Your Form ID
After creating the form, you'll see a page with:
```
Your form endpoint is:
https://formspree.io/f/XXXXX
```

**Copy the XXXXX part** (your Form ID)

**Example Form IDs:**
- `mkwopqxd`
- `xyzabc123`
- Your ID will be similar
